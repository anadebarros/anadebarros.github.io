About

I’m a science communicator from Lisbon with a solid experience in team management and strategic communication planning. Avid learner, curiosity and passion are my main driving forces to keep improving everyday.

I love to deconstruct the complexity of science so that others can fall in love with it like I do. I try to push my own limits and challenge myself everyday. Apart from film photography, I use all available digital tools for scientific dissemination. I excel in creative team environments.
